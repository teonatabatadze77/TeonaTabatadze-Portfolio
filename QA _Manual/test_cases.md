## ID: 134
**Title:** Add item to cart  
**Description:** Check whether the product is added to the cart and correctly retains the selected size and quantity.  
**Preconditions:** –  
**Postconditions:** The added product's name, size, and quantity are displayed correctly in the cart.  
**Priority:** High  
**Severity:** Major  
**Type:** Functional  
**Steps:**  
1. Open the main page  
2. Click on the "Women" section  
3. Click on the first product "Sports Top"  
4. Select size "S"  
5. Click "Add to Cart"  
**Expected Result:**  
- The page with products for women is displayed  
- The product page opens showing selectable options like size and color  
- The size is successfully selected  
- The product is successfully added to the cart with correct details
